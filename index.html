Our Heritage

A Legacy of Sweetness in Satara

Founded in 1950, Latkar Sweets started as a humble shop near Powai Naka. Today, we are synonymous with the "Taste of Satara". Our secret recipe for Kandi Pedha has been passed down through three generations, ensuring the same rich, caramelized flavor that our grandfather created.
